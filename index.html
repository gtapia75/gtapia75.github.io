MotorTrend – Warner Bros. Discovery
Enterprise Customer Data Platform (CDP) Transformation

Led a multi-year initiative to implement a Customer Data Platform (CDP) across MotorTrend's digital ecosystem, integrating analytics from eight different technology platforms including Web, Mobile Web, Android, iOS, AppleTV, Roku, Connected TVs and Gaming consoles.

Challenges & Complexity:

  • Managed integration across multiple teams (Data Engineering, Product, QA).
  • Consolidated data from multiple platforms into a single source of truth to improve accessibility and reduce reliance on Adobe Analytics. Created unified customer profiles by linking separate data points.
  • Integrated downstream systems including data warehouse, Google Ad Manager (GAM), internal email services, and visualization tools like Amplitude to enable deeper data analysis.
  • Addressed implementation bottlenecks by reducing dependence on a limited number of engineers with deep Adobe Analytics expertise, allowing for faster deployment and scalability.
  • Oversaw 1,500+ development tickets related to data integration, validation, and automation during the first 12 months.

Innovations & Solutions:

  • Introduced "Situation Rooms", a collaborative problem-solving framework that doubled ticket resolution speed and improved cross-team communication.
  • Built real-time data validation pipelines, reducing data inconsistencies by 75% and ensuring accuracy across platforms.
  • Developed automated dashboards that transformed reporting from days to minutes, providing instant access to key business metrics.
  • Enabled real-time tracking of Core Web Vitals, ad load times, and ad performance metrics, allowing teams to monitor ads requested, rendered, and viewed.

Impact & Achievements:

  • $750K in cost savings through workflow optimization and reduced dependency on Adobe Analytics.
  • Increased accessibility of analytics across the company, allowing teams in Sales, Ad Sales, Finance, Business Intelligence, Product Management, Program Management, QA, and Marketing to self-serve insights using Amplitude.
  • Created human-readable data attributes, eliminating the need for specialized data analysts to extract insights.
  • Enabled near real-time analytics, improving decision-making accuracy and speed across the organization.

This transformation unlocked company-wide data visibility, empowering all teams to make faster, data-driven decisions with more accessible and accurate analytics.
